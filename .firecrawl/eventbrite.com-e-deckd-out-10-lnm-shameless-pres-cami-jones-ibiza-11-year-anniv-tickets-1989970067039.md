About Cami Jones: Rooted in rhythm, soul, and pure dancefloor instinct, Cami Jones is a DJ, producer, and vocalist known for her deep, sultry fusion of house and techno—groove-heavy, emotionally charged, and impossible to ignore.Now based in Ibiza, Cami has become a trusted fixture on the island’s most iconic dancefloors, holding a residency at Pikes Ibiza, where her sets are known for their intimacy, unpredictability, and unforgettable energy. Whether warming up a terrace or commanding a peak-time room, she reads the crowd with precision—guiding dancers through raw, euphoric, and magnetic sonic journeys.

Her music has been released on some of the most respected labels in underground dance

music, including Hot Creations, Repopulate Mars, Kaluki, HE.SHE.THEY, and her own rising

imprint, nooghty. As both a producer and vocalist, Cami weaves hypnotic textures and sensual toplines that blur genre boundaries and captivate dancefloors worldwide.

What truly sets her apart is the emotional depth she brings to every performance. From

Australia’s coastlines to the heart of the European circuit, Cami has built a reputation for

crafting experiences—not just playing tracks. Her presence behind the decks is powerful,

authentic, and refreshingly unpretentious.

With a growing global following and a wave of new music and standout shows ahead in 2025, Cami Jones is already recognized as one of the most exciting and soulful voices in

underground dance music—a talent defined by substance, groove, and timeless style.
